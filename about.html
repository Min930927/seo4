Our Story

Vinyl Vibes was founded in 2026 with a simple mission: to bring the authentic sound of vinyl records back to music lovers worldwide. We believe that listening to a record is an experience, not just background noise.

Premium Quality

We source the highest quality vinyl pressings from around the globe, ensuring that every record you purchase from us delivers crystal clear audio and stunning album artwork.

Our Collection

From the latest K-Pop and J-Rock releases to timeless Western classics and underground Chinese indie, our curated collection is designed to satisfy every musical taste.
